Curriculum Details Guideline for Nursery to 5th Class

Nursery (Pre-K):

- Language Development: Introduction to letters, sounds, and
  basic vocabulary.
- Math Readiness: Counting, basic shapes, and colors.
- Motor Skills: Fine motor skills development through drawing,
  coloring, and play.
- Social Skills: Sharing, taking turns, and basic manners.
- Creative Arts: Exploration of art materials and basic
  creativity.
- Play-Based Learning: Structured play activities to promote cognitive and social development.

Kindergarten (K):

- Language Arts: Introduction to reading and writing, sight words,
  basic sentences.
- Maths: Number recognition, basic addition and subtraction,
  patterns.
- Science: Introduction to the natural world, basic concepts
  like seasons and animals.
- Social Studies: Basic understanding of community, families,
  and traditions.
- Fine Arts: Introduction to music, basic drawing and painting.
- Physical Education: Gross motor skill development through
  movement and games.
- Social Skills: Teamwork, communication, and sharing.

1st Grade to 3rd Grade:

- Language Arts: Reading comprehension, vocabulary building,
  creative writing.
- Maths: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, basic
  geometry.
- Science: Exploration of life, earth, and physical sciences.
- Social Studies: Local and world geography, history, cultures.
- Fine Arts: Music appreciation, more advanced drawing and
  crafts.
- Physical Education: Skill development in sports and physical
  activities.
- Critical Thinking: Problem-solving activities, puzzles, and
  basic logic.
- Character Education: Ethical values, empathy, and
  responsibility.

4th Grade to 5th Grade:

- Language Arts: Reading literature, writing essays, grammar rules.
- Maths: Fractions, decimals, more advanced geometry and algebra.
- Science: In-depth studies in various science disciplines
  (biology, chemistry, physics).
- Social Studies: Historical events, government, geography.
- Fine Arts: Introduction to drama, advanced art techniques.
- Physical Education: Skill-building, team sports, health
  education.
- Critical Thinking: Logical reasoning, critical analysis of
  texts.
- Technology Literacy: Basic computer skills, digital research,
  online safety.
